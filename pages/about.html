About me

My degree involved 4 years of study and 1 year in industry at Sumo Digital. The focus of my degree was low level C++ programming using various API's. I have the most experience with Vulkan and DX12, but I've also worked with DX11, PS4 and PS5.

My most notable university project involved a collaboration between Steel Minions (Sheffield Hallam University's game studio) and Sumo Digital Academy. Steel Minions produced 2 ports of Zool Redimensioned, 1 for PS4, and 1 for Vulkan which was the focus for my team. The project was a finalist at the TIGA UK Games Education Awards 2022, and the PS4 port was released in May 2023. Zool Redimensioned is special for me as it was my first credit.

My dissertation was recognised with the Outstanding Research Award at Game Over 2024, hosted by Sheffield Hallam University. I am also nominated for the Excellence in Games Research Award, and the Outstanding Postgraduate of the Year award, at the TIGA UK Games Education Awards 2024.
