I've worked at Sumo Digital since July 2024 as a Junior Programmer. I'm a generalist who can turn their hands to most things, and I have experience in gameplay, graphics, tools, UI, and 3C's programming. I am always keen to learn and to be the best programmer I can be, which I believe is demonstrated by the fact that I am a multiple finalist at TIGA, and I earned the TIGA Postgraduate of the Year Accolade in 2024.

I hold a first-class MCOMP in Computer Science for Games, which involved 4 years of study and 1 year in industry at Sumo Digital. The focus of my degree was low level C++ programming using various API's. I have the most experience with Vulkan and DX12, but I've also worked with DX11, PS4 and PS5.

I was part of a team of students at Steel Minions (Sheffield Hallam University's game studio) who ported Zool Redimensioned to PS4 and to Vulkan. The project was a finalist at the TIGA UK Games Education Awards 2022, and the PS4 port was released in May 2023. Zool Redimensioned is special for me as it was my first credit.

My dissertation focussed on improving fine motor skills in FPS players, and I developed my own take on motor-chunking theory. It was recognised with the Outstanding Research Award at Game Over 2024, hosted by Sheffield Hallam University, and it was a finalist for the TIGA Excellence in Games Research Award 2024.
