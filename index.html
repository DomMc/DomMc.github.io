Dom McCollum

My career in games began after 11 years in the rail industry. Adapting for a new career was challenging, particularly retraining after so long out of education, but I've enjoyed every day of it.

The focus of my degree was low level C++ programming using various API's. I have the most experience with Vulkan and DX12, but have worked with DX11, PS4 and PS5. My most notable university project involved a collaboration between Steel Minions (Sheffield Hallam University's game studio) and Sumo Digital Academy. Steel Minions produced 2 ports of Zool Redimensioned, 1 for PS4 and 1 for Vulkan. My team focused on the Vulkan port, and the project was a finalist at the TIGA UK Games Education Awards 2022. The PS4 port was released in May 2023 and is special for me as it was my first credit.
